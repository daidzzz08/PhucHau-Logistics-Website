Brand Story

The Journey of Building Trust

Origins from Understanding

Starting as young entrepreneurs in Da Nang, we understood the "pain points" of online shop owners: slow deliveries causing cancellations, and delayed COD returns affecting capital flow.

Phuc Hau Logistics was born with a simple but powerful mission: To become a reliable right-hand partner, making delivery easier and more trustworthy than ever.

"Vision: To become the #1 Intra-city Logistics Provider in Central Vietnam by 2027."
